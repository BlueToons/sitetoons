WHO AM I?

Hey there! I'm the creator of SiteToons, a digital artist and web designer with a passion for animation and creative expression. This website serves as my personal art hub where I share my creations, projects, and connect with other artists.

I've been drawing since I could hold a pencil and started digital art around 2018. My style has evolved over the years, but I've always been drawn to vibrant colors and expressive characters.
